Flowers photos

Flowers often have colorful petals, which attract pollinators like bees, butterflies, and birds. They typically consist of four main parts: Sepals – Protect the flower bud before it opens. Petals – Brightly colored to attract pollinators. Stamens – The male reproductive organs, producing pollen. Pistil – The female reproductive organ, containing the ovary where seeds develop.

Roses pictures

Roses come in various colors, each carrying different meanings, and they are widely used in gardens, bouquets, perfumes, and even skincare products. Characteristics of a Rose: Scientific Name: Rosa Family: Rosaceae Petals: Usually soft, layered, and fragrant Thorns: Most species have thorns on their stems for protection
